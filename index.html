24H Cake Timer

A minimalist 24-hour focus tracker that lets you log deep work sessions, tag slices, monitor streaks, and plan todos – all stored locally in your browser.

The central “cake” dial highlights work in color-coded segments, while daily and weekly reviews surface stats about your sessions, breaks, and goals.
